Beyond Prayers

Date: August 2, 2019

There is a growing epidemic. It is not the Ebola virus or a new strain of tuberculosis or influenza or any other infectious disease. It is not the growing discord among neighboring countries or hostile factions within countries or the influx of immigrants from disparate countries. It's even more insidious and can be easily eradicated. It is the issue of World Hunger

According to WorldHunger.org, "815 million people of the 7.6 billion people in the world or 10.7%" experienced extreme cases of chronic undernourishment. While the majority of the cases occur in lower-middle income countries, developed countries are not immune, 11 million cases having been documented within their borders.

The Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) of the United Nations recently published a documented confirming this information and urging for the remediation of this pertinent issue. In the document, "The State of Food Security and Nutrition in the World 2019" , the FAO states that hunger is on the rise and that a "little over 820 million people suffer from hunger." and that "maternal and child undernutrition contributes to 45 percent of deaths in children under five."

We need your help desperately to stem the rising tide of worldwide hunger by either becoming a Donor, donating monetarily to help us, or by becoming a Contributor, lending a helping hand to those people who need your help the most. For our Donors, we are a registered 501c(3) federally recognized nonprofit organization and every dollar you donate goes directly to provide the needed sustenance for people in the affected areas.

By: Staff Writer

Every child deserves to live a healthy life: to not have to worry about where they will receive their next meal

Children need to live in an environment rich with access to nutritionally dense food.

We can give every child a chance to live a healthy life and wake up every morning with a smile.
